Favorite Places to Visit According to Dhanya

My top 3 Places of all-time.

Hampi

This is my favourite place. I love the beautiful carvings and the amusing temples.It was built by Hakka and Bukka and Krishnadevaraya is my favourite emperor of the Vijaynagar kingdom

Rajasthan

Really cool to see the desert and heart warming welcome of the natives of Rajasthan.And the Hawa Mahal is jaw dropping

Hoganikkal Falls

Super beautiful Falls. Enjoy the cool river rides in Tamil nadu.
